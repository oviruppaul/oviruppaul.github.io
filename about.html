About Me

I am a Scientific Officer at the Bangladesh Livestock Research Institute (BLRI), specializing in Veterinary Epidemiology. My professional focus is dedicated to improving animal health systems and sustainable dairy production in Bangladesh.

Educational Background

I hold an MSc in Epidemiology (CGPA 3.89) and a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine (DVM) from Chattogram Veterinary and Animal Sciences University. My academic journey was further enriched by international clinical internships at Madras Veterinary College and Veterinary College & Research Institute, Namakkal in India.

International Training & Expertise

In 2020, I completed specialized training on "Effective Livestock Production with Low Use of Antibiotics" at the Swedish University of Agricultural Sciences (SLU). I also underwent intensive training in bacteriological diagnostics at the National Veterinary Institute (SVA), Sweden.

Technical Competence

I am proficient in advanced epidemiological tools including STATA, ArcGIS, and Epi Info. My technical skills extend to molecular and serological testing, data collection, and providing high-level training to veterinary practitioners and commercial farm owners.
